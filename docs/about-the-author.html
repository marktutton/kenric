Kenric Oswy is a native of and lives in Milwaukee.

He has authored several other books and scholarly articles under a different name. Kenric Oswy is a pen name for this book. He completed post-doctoral training at The University of Chicago.

The subject matter of this book is within his area of expertise.

He remains an active member and strong advocate of Milwaukee's LGBT community.
